DOGWOOD

10 June 2014

DOGWOOD is an SVG-based tree UI. In this demo, click on the red divs in the tree to add child nodes. Nodes with children have blue circles. Click on the blue circle of a node to toggle its childrens' visibility.

ambigui is a parent project for composable, dependency-free UI elements that do exactly what I want. It is MIT licensed.
